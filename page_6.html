Benefits
of Open Source Security Hardware

Faster Vulnerability Discovery

Open inspection enables rapid identification and remediation of security flaws by global experts

Community-Driven Improvements

Collaborative development accelerates innovation and improves design quality

Vendor-Neutral Design

Independent governance prevents vendor lock-in and ensures long-term sustainability

Avoids Proprietary Lock-In

Freedom to modify, integrate, and customize without licensing restrictions

Academic & Industry Research

Open designs enable reproducible research, verification, and security analysis
